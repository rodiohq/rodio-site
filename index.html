Rodio

The control plane for distributed intelligence.

Rodio is a research and engineering project studying large-scale orchestration for intelligent systems. It combines ideas from distributed systems (Raft, Ray) and reasoning frameworks to build deterministic, observable runtimes for multi-agent coordination.

The project focuses on reliability, clarity, and interpretability — treating orchestration as a systems-level challenge.
